Managing Director

A Managing Director has a strong business impact and is often part of the company's leadership. A Managing Director is capable of shaping a piece of business being the leader in a specific domain. A Managing Director develops high-level business relations and high-impact projects. A Managing Director is capable of leading large teams.

Senior Principal

A Senior Principal has demonstrated excellence in some competency. A Senior Principal is known for expertise and is capable of generating his or her own work and that of others. The Senior Principal is often responsible for business volume, through (add-on) sales and delivering a project. A Senior Principal can act as a team lead or counselor for other team members.

Principal

A Principal has developed a specialization within a specific domain of expertise. A Principal is capable of working independently as well as in teams. A Principal is often responsible for the completion of a part of a project or activities for which he/she leads a small team.

Associate

An Associate has demonstrated ability to elicit requirements and collect data. An Associate can perform analysis, support workshops, collaborate with SMEs, and is 'learning the trade'.

Analyst

An Analyst has an undergraduate degree and demonstrated ability to think critically. Has shown strong communication skills and a desire to achieve at a high level.
